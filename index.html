About Us

Posup Limited is a procurement and logistics company registered in Kenya and we have a diverse product and service portfolio.

Over the years, we have been involved in education and institutional infrastructure development in the Eastern Africa Region.

One of our flagship products and services includes provision of solar lighting solutions in the remote Northeastern Regions of Kenya and Eastern Africa Region.

We are a well experienced and dynamic team with over 15 years’ experience of industry expertise. Our Philosophy is one of partnership and flexibility.
